Welcome to Autofront

What is Autofront

  • 0-60 back office applications for managing your data
  • Accelarate full stack development for teams that lack frontend experience
  • No lock in - The data api made for this tool can easily be transitioned later on to drive a bespoke front end

Features

  • Dynamic Data display of business data
  • Triggering of Business flows
  • Themeable
  • Serverless and self contained
  • OIDC support - all security is passed through the app and handled by the backend so you decide what people can and cant see
